### 📊 Sprint Summary
* **Number of user stories planned:** 6
* **Number of user stories completed/delivered:** 7 
* **Sprint Outcome:** We successfully exceeded our planned story count. While the core features are deployed, we have identified specific UI and routing refinements needed for the next iteration.

---

### ✅ What Went Well
* **Code Review Efficiency:** Pull requests were reviewed and merged promptly, preventing bottlenecks in the development pipeline.
* **Project Transparency:** The GitHub board was updated in real-time, ensuring stakeholders had an accurate view of project health.
* **Reliability:** All committed stories were delivered successfully and ahead of schedule, allowing us to exceed our initial velocity.

---

### ⚠️ What Went Wrong
* **Environment Parity & Asset Loading:** Time was lost troubleshooting "it works on my machine" bugs. The live Render deployment is functional, but minor pathing fixes are still required to ensure all assets (images/CSS) render correctly across different environments.
* **Lack of Interconnectivity:** While individual pages (Landing, Login, Activity Log) were implemented and deployed, they were not all fully linked via navigation. Users currently have to navigate via direct URLs rather than a unified flow.
* **Deployment Configuration:** Initial deployment failed due to non-standardized strategies for serving static assets, resulting in MIME type errors during the final merge.

---

### 🚀 What Can Be Improved
* **Daily Integration:** Team members must start stories early and push to the repository daily. This avoids "integration hell" and ensures that deployment bugs are caught long before the sprint deadline.
* **Full-Stack Thinking:** Instead of focusing on stories in isolation, we need to ensure that "Done" includes the navigation and connectivity between pages.
* **Proactive Backlog Grooming:** If current sprint tasks are finished early, team members should immediately pull from the backlog to prevent a "pile-up" of work in later sprints.
* **Sprint 2 Immediate Action:** The first task of Sprint 2 will be a "Cleanup Spike" to finalize asset pathing and connect all existing pages into a single, cohesive user journey.

---

### 🏁 Conclusion
**Sprint 1 Status: COMPLETED** The foundation of the app is live. Sprint 2 will focus on connectivity, database integration, and UI polishing.
